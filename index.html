Our Story

Pizza Bliss started as a small family-owned pizzeria in the heart of Mian Channu. What began as a passion for authentic Italian pizza quickly became a local favorite.

Using only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes, we've been serving smiles since 2010. Our secret? A wood-fired oven that gives our pizzas that perfect crispy crust.

Today, we're proud to be part of Mian Channu's food culture, serving over 500 happy customers daily with the same love and care as day one.
